Human 'Beans'

Ideally, the human beings would never hurt one another. This is generally because, having experienced what pain feels like, we tend to not want to inflict it on others. So why do humans hurt each others? Well, it turns out pain is felt differently for different people and also responded to differently. Our lack of uniformity in experience also doesn't help matters Take for instance the woman the felt a lot of pain during childbirth.
